Dolenz

review

The Good Gay DOLENZ

Dolenz is a promising producer and DJ from London Neil Andrew. Presently he is little known (at least in Russia), but already managed to recommend himself as DJ Solo and Wriggly Scott.
As producer, Dolenz was spotted first by London-based Sonic Router label, which released first EP "Hysteresis" in 2015.

It turned out very futuristic and dynamic Hip-Hop release, after which the other London-based Exit Records label was interested in Andrew. After two years Dolenz had released EP "Evesdrop" on Exit Records, which became a certain trial version for label. Release got support from many electronic producers like: Lefto, Tim Parker, Shy One, Noisia, dBridge.

As far as known, currently Dolenz continues to work closely with Exit Records and is working on release his first LP at the beginning of 2019.
Also you can find him on the airwaves via NTS, Radar Radio or Balamii, where Dolenz is a resident and leads monthly radio show «Infinite Friends».
